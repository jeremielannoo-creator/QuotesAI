Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2025

1. Overview

QuotesAI is a personal automated content creation tool that generates and publishes motivational quote videos to social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram). This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles data.

2. Data We Collect

QuotesAI collects the minimum data necessary to function:

  • TikTok account information: We access only your TikTok user ID and display name to authenticate and publish content on your behalf.
  • Access tokens: OAuth tokens provided by TikTok are stored securely as environment variables and are used solely to publish video content.

3. How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok on your behalf
  • Upload and publish video content to your TikTok account

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party.

4. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored as encrypted GitHub Actions secrets. No personal data is stored in databases or external services beyond what TikTok retains as part of its own platform.

5. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as necessary to operate the service. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings under "Apps and Permissions".

6. Your Rights

You may revoke QuotesAI's access to your TikTok account at any time by visiting TikTok → Settings → Privacy → Apps and Websites.

7. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the GitHub repository: github.com/jeremielannoo-creator/QuotesAI
